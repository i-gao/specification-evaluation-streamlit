What Makes Us Special

🌾

Stone Ground

We use stone-milled organic flour from local mills, creating rich flavors and wholesome nutrition in every bite.

🥖

Long Fermentation

Our doughs rest and develop for up to 48 hours, creating complex flavors and easier digestion through natural processes.

🔥

Wood-Fired Ovens

Baked in our traditional brick ovens, each loaf gets the perfect crust and tender crumb that only fire can create.

Our Daily Breads & Pastries

Sourdough Country Loaf

Our signature bread with a crispy crust and open crumb. Made with 100-year-old starter and fermented for 24 hours.

Whole Grain Seeded

Hearty multigrain bread studded with sunflower, pumpkin, and sesame seeds. Perfect for sandwiches or toasted with butter.

Honey Oat Sandwich

Soft, tender crumb sweetened with local honey and rolled oats. A family favorite for every meal.

Rosemary Olive Focaccia

Fluffy Italian flatbread with fresh rosemary and Kalamata olives. Drizzled with olive oil and finished with flaky salt.

Cinnamon Swirl

Buttery brioche dough layered with cinnamon sugar and topped with cream cheese glaze. Weekend mornings made better.

Rustic Baguette

Classic French bread with a crackling crust and airy interior. Baked fresh three times daily.
