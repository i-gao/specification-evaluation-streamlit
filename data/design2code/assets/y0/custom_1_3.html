Our Story

At Crumb, we believe that bread is life, and every pastry should be a moment of pure joy. Our story began with a passion for the art of baking and a commitment to doing things the right way.

Since 2018, we've been waking up before dawn to craft small-batch artisan breads and pastries using time-honored techniques and locally-sourced ingredients.

We never rush the process. Our doughs rise slowly, our croissants are hand-laminated, and our sourdough starter has been lovingly maintained for generations. The result? Baked goods that taste like they should—rich, complex, and utterly delicious.
